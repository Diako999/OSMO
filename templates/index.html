What is Osmo

OSMO is the native Osmosis token, and it underpins the entire Osmosis protocol, and it facilitates everything from liquidity mining reward allocation and the base network swap fee. As a governance token, OSMO enables holders to decide the strategic direction of the project. It can be used to vote on upgrades to the protocol, allocate liquidity mining rewards for specific liquidity pools, and set the base network swap fee. OSMO token holders determine which pools are eligible for liquidity rewards with the goal of aligning stakeholders and LPs with the longevity of the protocol. OSMO is unique in that it can be used to stake and provide liquidity at the same time. The OSMO tokenomics are as follows: At genesis, an initial supply of 100 million OSMO, split between Fairdrop recipients and strategic reserve. Osmos has events in which new tokens are released called “daily epochs.” The newly released tokens are distributed as follows: Staking Rewards: 25% Developer Vesting: 25% Liquidity Mining Incentives: 45% Community Pool: 5%

HOW DOES OSMOSIS WORK?

Osmosis is secured by a proof of work consensus protocol with a validator set. Osmosis works on a similar protocol to Bitcoin. Like Bitcoin has a ‘Halving’ scheduled, Osmosis token issuance follows a “thirdening” schedule that cuts the issuance each year. The dex also has customizable liquidity pools. Osmosis offers a flexible solution that allows them to self-identify opportunities and react to them by adjusting parameters. Users can adjust the slippage, transaction fees, and more. The platform offers equal rights to all the participants to make the defi market more inclusive with fractional ownership. The users actively participate in the decision making to make the platform secure from vampire attacks.

WHAT MAKES OSMOSIS UNIQUE?

Osmosis, a dex built on the IBC protocol, is different from other decentralized exchanges as it offers single liquidity staking in different size ratios making it easy for anyone to become a liquidity provider. Secondly, the decentralized exchange in itself is a huge ecosystem for IBC linked dApps as well as solutions that are outside the Cosmos ecosystem. Secondly, the exchange is fully customizable. Meaning the users have more control and flexibility to stake and earn APY. In addition to earning interest on their idle assets, the user can also participate in the governance of the decentralized exchange. Each user gets to voice their opinion on any decision or proposal laid for the development of the protocol. The platform also introduces the idea of “AMMs as serviced infrastructure’’ Automated Market Makers have been facing increased complexity in the system which Osmosis tries to solve by providing AMM creators with an option to define the bonding curve value function and reuse the rest of the infrastructure using Osmosis’ products.
